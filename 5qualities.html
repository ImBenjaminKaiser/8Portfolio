The Five Qualities

Collaboration

Collaboration is when people work together as a team to achieve a common goal.

Communication

Communication is the ability to clearly express your ideas through speech, writing, or body language.

Creativity

Creativity is the skill of taking existing ideas and concepts and transforming them into something new and innovative.

Persistence

Persistence means not giving up even when you face challenges and continuing to find a solution even when it seems like you can't.

Inquiry

Inquiry is bring curious and asking questions to further your understanding and knowledge of a certain concept.
